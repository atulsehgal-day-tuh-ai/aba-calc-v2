1. Welcome & Quick Start

What This System Does

The ABA Medical Necessity Calculator is a web-based decision support tool that brings transparency and consistency to ABA therapy authorization. It answers the question every stakeholder asks:

"How many hours of ABA therapy does this patient need per week?" The system uses a clinically-grounded, 7-step algorithm to produce a transparent, reproducible recommendation ‚Äî and both clinics and insurers see the same math.

üõ°Ô∏è Insurance Payer Users

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why doesn't the calculation update?

A: Enter a valid Age (2‚Äì21) in Section 1. The calculation won't start until age is provided.

Q: Can I save a partially completed assessment?

A: In the current version, assessments aren't saved until submitted. Complete the full assessment in one session.

Q: Why does a teenager get fewer hours than a young child with the same scores?

A: The age multiplier gives a 20% boost for children ‚â§5 (early intervention) and a 15% reduction for teens (who get school supports). Exception: High-risk patients bypass the teen reduction entirely.

Q: What does the ML Predictor actually predict?

A: It estimates the likelihood that a typical insurer would approve the request, based on the completeness and severity of clinical data. It's a decision-support tool, not a guarantee.

Q: Can payer profile changes affect existing claims?

A: No. Existing claims keep their original calculation. Profile changes only affect future calculations. Use the Policy Calculator for scenario testing.

Q: Is there an audit trail?

A: Yes. Every submission, status change, and review action is logged with timestamps and user roles.

Q: How do I interpret clinical flags?

A: Flags are automatic alerts that highlight situations requiring special attention. They don't change the calculation but signal the need for additional documentation or safety planning.
